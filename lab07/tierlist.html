Reflection

For this list, I decided to look back at some old gaming memories. I've always liked SEGA so I focused on games I've played from that company. The games are ranked from my most favorite ("S" tier) to my least favorite ("D" tier).

I find that organizing tables isn't too difficult. I surprised myself a little because I didn't need to check my notes or online guides to create the HTML portion of the table. However, I did need to check my notes and online resources like w3schools when it came to styling.
